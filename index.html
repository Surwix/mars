◆ FAQ ◆
Questions

Frequently Asked

Is this real land ownership on Mars?+
MarsLand certificates are novelty gift certificates — similar to star naming registries. No current legal framework allows private Mars ownership under the 1967 Outer Space Treaty. Your number, coordinates, and registry entry are permanent and unique.
Why do some numbers cost more?+
Numbers #001–#100 are priced by rarity — just like early domain names or license plates. #001 is the first Mars citizen ever. Once purchased, that number can never belong to anyone else. Standard numbers (#101+) are assigned automatically and start at $9.
How is my number guaranteed unique?+
Numbers are assigned via auto-increment in our database, triggered only after Stripe payment is confirmed. Premium numbers are reserved instantly when you begin checkout. The SHA-256 hash on your certificate cryptographically proves it's yours.
Can I verify my certificate is authentic?+
Yes — every certificate has a QR code linking to marslandspace.com/verify.html. Anyone can scan to confirm your name, coordinates, and registration date. Your SHA-256 hash is also printed on the certificate and cannot be forged.
Can I buy this as a gift?+
Absolutely — one of the most unique gifts for space fans. Enter the recipient's name during checkout and we'll send the certificate to their email.
What is your refund policy?+
30-day money-back guarantee on digital certificates. Physical merch returnable within 14 days if unused.
