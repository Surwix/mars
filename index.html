◆ FAQ ◆
Questions

Frequently Asked

Is this real land ownership on Mars?+
MarsLand certificates are novelty gift certificates — the same concept as star naming registries. No current legal framework allows private Mars ownership under the 1967 Outer Space Treaty. Your number, coordinates, and registry entry are permanent and real.
How is my citizen number guaranteed unique?+
Your number is assigned via auto-increment in our database, triggered only after Stripe payment is confirmed. Mathematically impossible for two people to share a number. The SHA-256 hash cryptographically proves authenticity.
Can I verify my certificate is authentic?+
Yes — every certificate has a QR code linking to marslandspace.com/verify/[your-number]. Anyone can scan to confirm your name, coordinates, and registration date.
Can I buy this as a gift?+
Absolutely — one of the most unique gifts for space fans. Enter the recipient's name during checkout and we'll generate the certificate in their name.
What is your refund policy?+
30-day money-back guarantee on digital certificates. Physical merch returnable within 14 days if unused.
